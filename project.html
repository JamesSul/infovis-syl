Final Project - Winter Olympics

The 2014 winter olympics in Sochi is now complete. Here is a data file of all medals awarded in Sochi. You must find and use additional country specific data (e.g., economic, demographic, geographic, weather, etc.), historic winter olympic data, and tell a compelling visual story, with narrative, of what contributes to a country's success in the 2014 winter olympics. You will likely need to present multiple visualizations to accomplish this.

In addition, I want you to show whether or not your story holds water in light of prior olympic data (this dataset includes all winter olympics through 2006 -- you will need to find the 2010 data on your own).

You may use Piazza for your narrative but you might find it suboptimal since you can't embed interactive graphics. There are plenty of free blogging sites where you can accomplish this. Alternatively you may use Google Sites with your maine.edu account. If you do use Google Sites, the way you need to embed the Tableau vis isn't perfectly straightforward. You'll need to edit the html code and add something that looks like this:

				
	<iframe style="border: 0px;" 
		src="<insert your link here>"
		scrolling="no"
		width="652px"
		height="756px">
	</iframe>

You'll be pasting your link (not script) to your Tableau vis in-between the quotes after src=". Simply put, an iframe is an html embedded document inside another html document. Feel free to modify the width, height, and scrolling settings as well...those are just an example. Another alternative would be to create a Youtube or Vimeo video with you audio narrating your story (you'll still need to post public server links to Piazza).

Just like in your assignments, your narrative should include the story you are telling and the rationale for the visual encodings you chose. In addition, you'll need to provide: * The source data you used (with links). * A description of the process you used to clean/merge your data. * A description of any issues you encountered while completing this assignment. * Any other data you wish you could have had but you were unable to find. * Any topics I might have covered but didn't that would have made this assignment easier for you.

If you choose to not use Piazza for your narrative, post a link to your narrative with embedded visualizations in Piazza. Make sure you test everything before you submit.
