Final Unit

This unit is similar to the units where we have had an opportunity to reflect and more time to thoughtfully critique assignments. I’m not going to assign any additional readings and I want you to take the time to fully critique other student’s projects. For your specific assignment, any critique that was provided prior to end of day 8/13, I fully expect you to either address it in the Piazza comments or modify your visualization(s). Please try to avoid non-committal responses like “that is a great suggestion, next time I’ll try doing that.”

Also, please keep in mind that any responses to critiques of your work or modifications you make from critiques are due end of day 8/15.
